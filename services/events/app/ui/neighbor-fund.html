The Neighbor Fund

Some weeks are hard. The Neighbor Fund lets you round up a little at checkout so someone nearby who's having one of those weeks still gets a hot meal brought right to their door.

How it works
1

Round up when you order

At the end of any order, add a dollar or two — or whatever you like. It's never required and never assumed.

2

It pools into the fund

Every round-up goes into one shared pot. Each time it reaches the cost of a standard delivery ($5.99), that's one more delivery we can cover.

3

A neighbor's delivery gets covered

When someone tells us they're in a tight spot — a hard week, a fixed income, a rough stretch — the fund quietly covers their delivery fee. No forms to prove they deserve it.

Our promise on this money

  • GateWay takes nothing. Not a fee, not a cut, not a cent. 100% of every round-up covers a real delivery.
  • It only ever pays the delivery fee — the driver is still paid in full, always.
  • No account needed to give, and no one is charged automatically. You choose, every time.
