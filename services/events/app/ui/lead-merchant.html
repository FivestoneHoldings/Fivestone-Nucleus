Questions kitchens actually ask
What does it cost?

No signup fee, no monthly fee. We take a commission per delivered order — we'll walk through the exact number with you before you ever go live, in plain terms.

Do I need my own delivery drivers?

No — GateWay drivers cover your deliveries. If you already have your own driver, we can route around them instead; tell us what setup works for you.

Can I change my menu myself?

Yes, anytime, through your own kitchen screen — no waiting on us to push an update. Ran out of an item mid-shift? One tap and it's off the menu.

What if I already have a POS system?

Tell us what you use — we're not trying to replace it, just add a delivery channel on top of what already works for you.

How fast can I actually go live?

The bottleneck is almost always the menu — the faster we get real pricing from you, the faster you're live. Some kitchens have gone live in under a week.
