Where GateWay is headed

Everything live today — the storefront, tracking, the Neighbor Fund, driver profiles — is real and running. This page is different: it's where we're taking the platform next.

We're not going to show you a button that doesn't work. Nothing on this page is live in the app yet. This is the roadmap, presented honestly as a roadmap.
Pillar 1

🐝 Swarm Ordering

Group-buying for real-world gatherings. Drop a pin at a tailgate, an office park, a block party — anyone nearby joins the same order from the same kitchen, and the more people join, the lower everyone's delivery fee drops. One driver, one trip, one big order — instead of ten separate ones competing for the same kitchen's attention.

Why it wins: the kitchen gets a single efficient ticket instead of a flood of small ones, the driver makes one high-yield trip, and neighbors get a price break for ordering together.
Pillar 2

🗺️ Routing You Can See

At checkout, you'd choose how your order travels — plainly, no hidden batching. A discounted route where your order rides along with a couple of nearby neighbors' orders, shown to you on the map exactly as it'll run. Or a direct route, your order and only your order, for a bit more.

Why it wins: you always know what you're paying for and why — never a mystery fee for a routing decision made behind the scenes.
Pillar 3

🚗 The Driver Revolution

Zone Mayors — a driver who owns a neighborhood becomes the familiar face for its kitchens, with a share of that zone's growth as it's built. Fair-shift bonuses — on top of 100%-to-driver tips, drivers who run a clean, on-time shift earn platform-funded bonuses stacked on top, never pulled from a customer's tip.

Why it wins: drivers build something that's actually theirs in a neighborhood, instead of being an anonymous dot on someone else's map.
