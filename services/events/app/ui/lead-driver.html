Real talk — what people ask
Do I need my own car?

Yes — any reliable vehicle that can hold food safely. No specific make or year requirement; we care that it runs and the food stays upright.

Is this full-time or side money?

Whatever you want it to be. Some drivers do a couple of runs after work, some build most of their week around it. No minimum hours, ever.

How do I actually get paid?

Tips are cash (or noted) in your hand the moment you deliver — 100% yours, always. Base pay per run is set with dispatch when you're onboarded.

What if something goes wrong on a run?

Call dispatch. A real person answers — not a form, not a bot, not a 72-hour email queue.

Can I say no to a run?

Yes. No penalty, no score dinged, no algorithm quietly punishing you later. It's genuinely fine.
